Placement
Popovers can be positioned on the: top , right , bottom , and left . 
  
  {{'<'}}!--
    Valid settings are: "top", "bottom", "left", "right", "auto", "auto top", "auto bottom", "auto left", "auto right"
  -->
  Popovers can be positioned on the:
  {{'<'}}strong class="color-aqua" supreTooltip="Title Goes Here" popoverPlacement="top"> top {{'<'}}/strong>,
  {{'<'}}strong class="color-aqua" supreTooltip="Title Goes Here" popoverPlacement="right"> right {{'<'}}/strong>,
  {{'<'}}strong class="color-aqua" supreTooltip="Title Goes Here" popoverPlacement="bottom"> bottom {{'<'}}/strong>, and
  {{'<'}}strong class="color-aqua" supreTooltip="Title Goes Here" popoverPlacement="left"> left {{'<'}}/strong>
Disable
Popovers can be disabled/enabled: Enabled, Disabled. 
  
  {{'<'}}!-- disabled is optional, it defaults to 'false' -->
  {{'<'}}strong class="color-aqua" supreTooltip="Title Goes Here Enabled"> Enabled {{'<'}}/strong>,
  {{'<'}}strong class="color-aqua" [disabled]="true" supreTooltip="Title Goes Here Disabled"> Disabled {{'<'}}/strong>.
